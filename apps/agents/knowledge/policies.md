# GoBuddy Adventures - Company Policies

## Booking Policy

### Reservation Requirements
- Full payment is required at the time of booking
- All travelers must provide valid passport information
- Bookings are confirmed within 24 hours via email

### Group Bookings
- Groups of 6+ travelers receive a 10% discount
- Group bookings require a 30% deposit
- Final payment due 30 days before departure

## Cancellation Policy

### Refund Schedule
- **14+ days before trip start**: Full refund minus $50 processing fee
- **7-14 days before trip start**: 50% refund
- **Less than 7 days before trip start**: No refund available
- **No-show**: No refund

### Trip Credit
- Cancellations within 7 days may be eligible for trip credit
- Trip credits are valid for 12 months
- Credits are non-transferable

### Force Majeure
In case of natural disasters, pandemics, or other force majeure events:
- Full refund or complimentary rebooking offered
- Decision made case-by-case by management

## Travel Insurance

### Recommended Coverage
- We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance
- Coverage should include:
  - Trip cancellation/interruption
  - Medical emergencies
  - Lost luggage
  - Flight delays

### Partner Insurance
We partner with World Nomads for travel insurance.
Use code GOBUDDY10 for 10% discount.

## Payment Methods

### Accepted Payments
- Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (for bookings over $5,000)

### Currency
- All prices listed in USD
- Local currency conversions available at checkout
- Exchange rates updated daily

## Health & Safety

### COVID-19 Protocols
- Follow local destination guidelines
- Masks provided if required by local regulations
- Hand sanitizer available on all group tours

### Emergency Contacts
- 24/7 Emergency Line: +1-800-GO-BUDDY-911
- Local emergency numbers provided in trip documents
- Driver/guide emergency contact in app

### Medical Requirements
- No vaccinations required by GoBuddy
- Check destination-specific requirements
- Inform us of any medical conditions before travel

## Driver & Guide Standards

### Driver Requirements
- All drivers are licensed and insured
- Background checks completed
- Minimum 3 years professional driving experience

### Service Standards
- English-speaking drivers available
- Meet and greet at arrival point
- 24/7 driver availability during trip

## Complaints & Feedback

### During Trip
- Contact your driver/guide immediately
- Use in-app support chat
- Call emergency line for urgent issues

### After Trip
- Submit feedback via app within 7 days
- Email: feedback@gobuddy.com
- Response within 48 business hours

### Compensation
- Service issues may qualify for partial refund
- Future trip discounts offered for inconveniences
- Each case reviewed individually
