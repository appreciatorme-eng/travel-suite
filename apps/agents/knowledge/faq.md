# GoBuddy Adventures - Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking & Reservations

### How do I book a trip?
1. Browse destinations in the app or website
2. Select your dates and preferences
3. Choose activities and accommodations
4. Complete payment
5. Receive confirmation email within 24 hours

### Can I customize my itinerary?
Yes! All our trips are fully customizable. Contact us at custom@gobuddy.com or use the "Customize" button on any trip page.

### How far in advance should I book?
- Peak season (holidays, summer): 2-3 months ahead
- Regular season: 2-4 weeks ahead
- Last-minute bookings accepted subject to availability

### Do you offer gift cards?
Yes! Gift cards are available in any amount from $50-$5,000. They never expire and can be used for any trip.

## Payments & Pricing

### What's included in the trip price?
Typically includes:
- Accommodation
- Private driver/transportation
- Activities as listed
- 24/7 support

Not included (unless specified):
- International flights
- Meals (unless stated)
- Travel insurance
- Personal expenses

### Are there any hidden fees?
No hidden fees! The price you see is the price you pay. Taxes and service charges are included.

### Can I pay in installments?
For trips over $1,000, we offer:
- 3-month payment plan (0% interest)
- 6-month payment plan (2.5% fee)
Contact finance@gobuddy.com for details.

## Transportation & Drivers

### Are drivers English-speaking?
We offer English-speaking drivers in most destinations. Specify your language preference when booking.

### What type of vehicles do you use?
- Solo/Couple: Sedan or SUV
- Family (3-5): SUV or minivan
- Group (6+): Van or bus
All vehicles are air-conditioned and insured.

### Can I request a specific driver?
Yes, if you've traveled with us before. Include driver preference in your booking notes.

### What if my flight is delayed?
Drivers track your flight and adjust pickup time automatically. No extra charge for delays up to 3 hours.

## During Your Trip

### How do I contact my driver?
- Driver's WhatsApp number in the app
- Direct call button in trip details
- 24/7 support chat if driver unreachable

### What if I want to change my itinerary during the trip?
Minor changes can be made through your driver. Major changes:
- Contact support via app
- Changes subject to availability
- Additional costs may apply

### Is Wi-Fi available during transfers?
Many of our premium vehicles have Wi-Fi. Request this when booking. Standard vehicles may not have Wi-Fi.

### What about tipping?
Tipping is appreciated but not required. Suggested amounts:
- Drivers: $5-10/day
- Tour guides: $10-20/day
- Hotel staff: $1-2/service

## Accommodations

### What types of hotels do you use?
We work with 3-5 star hotels and quality boutique properties. You can:
- Choose from our curated selection
- Request specific hotels
- Mix hotel categories during trip

### Can I earn hotel loyalty points?
Yes! Book directly through us and:
- Provide your loyalty number
- Points credited to your account
- Works with most major chains

## Safety & Support

### What happens in an emergency?
1. Contact your driver immediately
2. Call our 24/7 emergency line
3. We coordinate with local authorities
4. Medical evacuation assistance if needed

### Is travel insurance required?
Not required but STRONGLY recommended. We offer partnership rates with World Nomads.

### How do you vet your drivers?
- Background checks
- Driving record verification
- Minimum 3 years experience
- Regular vehicle inspections
- Customer rating system

## After Your Trip

### How do I leave a review?
- In-app prompt after trip ends
- Email survey sent within 3 days
- Google/TripAdvisor reviews appreciated!

### I forgot something in the vehicle!
Contact support immediately with:
- Your booking reference
- Description of item
- Driver's name

We'll coordinate with the driver to return your item.

### How do I book another trip?
- Log in to your account
- Browse new destinations
- Past travelers get 5% loyalty discount!
